# IncomeOut
A simple, free personal finance tool to help you track income, manage expenses, and work towards your financial goals.

## Overview
IncomeOut is a personal finance management tool built with simplicity in mind. It's designed to give you a clear view of your finances without overwhelming complexity. Track your income, monitor expenses, set savings goals, and gain insights into your spending patterns—all in one place.

## Development Status
⚠️ **Note:** IncomeOut is currently in active development. While the core features are functional, you may encounter occasional bugs or missing features. Your feedback is valuable in shaping the future of this tool!

## Privacy Commitment

### What Data We Collect
- Your email address for account creation
- Financial data you enter to track income, expenses, and goals

### What We Don't Do
- ❌ Never sell your data
- ❌ Never share your information
- ❌ Never use your data for advertising

### Your Control
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.
